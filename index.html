Frequently asked questions

  1. What will happen to my Cloudflare data?

    2. All Cloudflare data, including domains, DNS records, subscriptions, etc, will be systematically deleted from the Cloudflare, Inc servers on July 27, 2021.

  2. Will there be any way to retrieve my data in the future?

    3. No -- all subscription data will be permanently, and irrevocably deleted. We will not be able to recover any Cloudflare subscription data for any user after July 27, 2021.

  3. Why was Cloudflare discontinued?

    4. Increased network attacks, as well as lack of funding and cutbacks due to COVID-19.
